Those who when they are receiving any measure from the people, they take it in full. [FreeMinds] ‌[83:2]

Ki onlar insanlardan alırken ölçüyü tam yaparlar, [Nuri] ‌[83:2]

А когда для них отмеривают им или отвешивают, мерят не верно. [Саблуков] ‌[83:3]
And when they are the ones giving measure or weight, they give less than due. [FreeMinds] ‌[83:3]
